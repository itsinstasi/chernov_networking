First time in Siberia.

Altay, Altayskiy Kray, Russia
29 June 2016
0 0

First time in Siberia. Altay turned out an amazing place! Not only the nature but also the infrastructure is quite ok (surprisingly). Mountains, pine forests, waterfalls but my favourite part is rafting in fast and furious Katun river. Dangerous activity but SO much fun!
